this note records the existing terrain. The honest goal is **not** to write a
comprehensive review — it is to read enough to make non-trivial choices in

known.

## §2 — Reeb-like generators on null hypersurfaces

**Status:** □ open

**What we need to know:**

- On a null hypersurface H, the standard "generator" is a null geodesic field ℓ.
- When H is a horizon, ℓ is hypersurface-orthogonal and shear-free in the
  isolated-horizon case (Ashtekar).
- We need: does ℓ play the role of R_α for a Lorentzian contact form α
  restricted to H?

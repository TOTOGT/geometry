What the workshops are

The Cajueiro de Pirangi is one organism. A single root system beneath 8,500 square meters of canopy. From the outside it looks like a forest. It is not a forest. It is one tree that learned to grow sideways.

The Cajueiro Principle — the organizing idea behind all of this — is that the most important structures in any living system are built underground, in silence, before anyone arrives to watch. The workshops are built around that idea.

Five workshops · November 2026 · Elizabeth NJ

  • The Cajueiro Principle and the mathematics of living systems
  • Academic English for researchers — bilingual Portuguese/English
  • The dm³ framework as a tool for reading scientific papers
  • Walking meditation along the proposed Ice & Iron Greenway
  • Sound and resonance — what 111 Hz does to a room

They are free because they need to be free. The target community is the immigrant corridor of Newark and Elizabeth — researchers, practitioners, and anyone for whom the roots of their work are not yet visible.
